Leadership

Cabinet Members

Cabinet Members within ABA are responsible for leading their team of Analysts as they work on large, client based projects, as well as provide necessary guidance and professional development for internships, jobs, and other opportunities.

Read more below to learn more about what each Cabinet Member does!

Senior Analysts

Senior Analysts within ABA focus on problem solving -- driven through dynamic team synergy, strong aptitude for learning, and creating innovative solutions throughout various project teams.

Account Managers

Account Managers within ABA lead client project acquisition and club-wide professional development to maximize the skills and experiences analysts are getting each semester.
